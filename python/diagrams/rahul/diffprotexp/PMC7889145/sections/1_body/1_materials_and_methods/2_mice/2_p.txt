0: All animal experiments were performed under the guidelines established by the Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC) of Virginia Commonwealth University.
1: Mice were housed in environmentally controlled conditions with a 12-h light/dark cycle (0600 lights on; 1800 lights off) and had free access to food and water.
2: Breeding colonies and experimental mice were maintained in a non-barrier facility with Teklad (Indianapolis, IN) non-irradiated diet 7019.
3: Adipoq-Cre mice (C57Bl/6/J background) were purchased from The Jackson Laboratory (Stock #028020) and then maintained and bred in house.
4: These mice were also crossed to  Adam10 floxed mice (C57Bl/6/J background) [ 7].
