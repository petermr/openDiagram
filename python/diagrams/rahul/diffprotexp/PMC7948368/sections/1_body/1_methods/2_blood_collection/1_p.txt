0: Pre-treatment and 3-month post-treatment blood samples were collected into TempusTM Blood RNA Tubes (3 ml) (Applied Biosystems, Foster City, CA, USA).
1: Once collected, samples were shipped to the Versus Arthritis Centre for Genetics and Genomics laboratory for central processing.
2: Samples were logged onto the laboratory information management system (LIMS) and were stored at  80 C until RNA isolation.
