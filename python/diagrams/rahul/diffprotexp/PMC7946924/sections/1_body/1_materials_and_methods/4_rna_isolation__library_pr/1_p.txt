0: Total RNA was extracted from nearly 50 mg fresh weight of rice spikelets after using Spectrum Plant Total RNA Kit (Cat# STRN50-1KT, Sigma) following manufacturers instruction.
1: For removal of residual DNA, on-column DNase (Cat#79254, Qiagen RNase free DNase) treatment was done and RNA was eluted in nuclease free water (Cat#AM9939, Ambion, USA).
2: The quantification and quality of the RNA was assessed using Nanodrop 2000 (Thermo Scientific, USA), Qubit (Thermo Scientific, USA) and Bioanalyzer 2100 (Agilent, USA).
