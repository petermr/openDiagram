1: The location of tumor occurrence in 10 patients included six cases of the mouths fundus, 4 cases of the gingiva.

4: All patients underwent surgery prior to chemotherapy or radiotherapy.
5: All samples were used for immunohistochemical staining.
6: Our study was conducted following the Declaration of Helsinki and approved by the Chinese PLA General Hospitals Ethics Committee.
