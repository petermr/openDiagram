0: Total RNA quality was estimated based on RNA integrity number (RIN) using Agilent BioAnalyzer 2100TM.
1: RNA samples with a RIN>8 was used for library preparation.

3: Samples were barcoded, multiplexed and sequenced (100 bp pair end) using the Illumina NextSeq 550 platform at NYU Abu Dhabi (NYUAD) Genomic Core facility (Abu Dhabi, U.A.E).
