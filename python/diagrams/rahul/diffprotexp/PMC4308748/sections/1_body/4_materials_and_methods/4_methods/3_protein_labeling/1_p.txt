0: The protein sample was adjusted to a final concentration of 5 mg/mL and adjusted to pH 8.09.0.
1: Each sample (50 g, 10 L) was used for analysis.
2: The gel electrophoresis dye was mixed for 30 seconds, centrifuged at 12 000  g for 30 seconds and left in place at room temperature for 10 minutes.
3: Then, 0.4 L dye and 0.6 L dimethylformamide were mixed together.
4: Subsequently, 50 g protein sample was added.
5: Following addition of sample buffer, the mixture was fully mixed and a hydration solution was added till the final volume was 450 L.
