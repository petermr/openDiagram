0: A total of 63 (septic) children diagnosed by pathology and evaluated by SOFA score system in Tianjin People's Hospital from January 2018 to December 2020 were randomly selected and collected.
1: Due to the limitation of sample size as well as in order to generate statistically convincing results, the patients were divided into three groups (control group: pSOFA = 0; mild pediatric sepsis group: pSOFA = 24; severe pediatric sepsis group: pSOFA score  5).
2: There were 21 cases in each group.
3: This study is in line with the medical ethics standards and approved by the hospital ethics committee.
4: All treatment and testing were carried out after obtaining informed consent from patients or their families.
