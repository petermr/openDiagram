0: This study conformed to the Guide for the Care and Use of Laboratory Animals by the National Institutes of Health (NIH Publication, 8th Edition, 2011) and China Animal Welfare Law.
1: The Wenzhou Medical University Committee on Ethics in the Use and Care of Laboratory Animals approved conduct of this study.
2: Male BALB/c mice (SPF class, 2024 g, 4 to 6 weeks) were purchased from Shanghai Laboratory Animal Center, China.
3: All mice were maintained in the Wenzhou Medical University animal facilities under specific pathogen-free conditions.
4: All experimental animals were sacrificed with an overdose of pentobarbital (100 mg/kg, one dose intraperitoneally).
