Results of survival analyses of hub genes and gender in TCGA-HNSC patients with an OS >5 years. (A, B) Neither CCR8 nor P2RY14 gene expression levels in patients with survival beyond 5 years were significantly associated with prognosis (P>0.05). (C) The patients did not exhibit survival-related gender differences (P>0.05).
