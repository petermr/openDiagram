0: All data were analysed using SPSS 13.0 (SPSS Inc., Chicago, IL, USA).
1: Data were expressed as means  standard deviations (SD), and analysed by a two-way ANOVA for two independent variables and by a one-way ANOVA for one variable among treatments.
2: Least significant difference (LSD) test was used for multiple comparisons.
3: The results were considered significant at  p < 0.05 (two-sided).
