0: The values in this study are expressed as mean  SD.
1: The Shapiro-Wilk test was used for verification of the normality of the data.
2: The Kruskal-Wallis test with multiple comparisons of average ranks was used to determine differences between samples with a nonnormal distribution.
3: The one-way analysis of variance (ANOVA) and the subsequent Tukey post hoc test were used to evaluate the differences between samples with a normal distribution.
4: The results were analyzed using STATISTICA 12.0 software (StatSoft, Poland).
5: Differences of  p< 0.05 were considered statistically significant.
