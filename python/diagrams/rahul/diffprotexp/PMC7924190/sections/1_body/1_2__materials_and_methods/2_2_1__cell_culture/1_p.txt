0: Telomerase-immortalized human corneal epithelial cell line [ 30] (hTCEpi; a kind gift from Prof. J. V. Jester, University of California, Irvine, CA, USA; authenticated and characterized according to ATCC standard protocols) between passages 24 and 30 was used in this present study.
