Patient enrollment, study design and different clinical parameters of COVID-19 patients. a Summary of recruited COVID-19 patients, including 18 severe patients, 4 critical survivors, and 11 critical non-survivors. b Admission days and multi-omics sample collection time of COVID-19 patients.
