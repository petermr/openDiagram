0: Male SpragueDawley albino rats weighing ~250 g were acquired from the Animal Center of Chongqing Medical University (China).
1: The animal protocols of this study were approved by the local Ethics Committee.
2: These animals were cared for following the National Institutes of Health protocols for the use and care of laboratory animals.
3: All animals were individually housed and maintained on a 12 h light/dark cycle with temperatures from 21 to 22 C, and relative humidity, 55  5%.
4: The animals were allowed to have access to food and water ad libitum.
