Protein–protein interaction and parallel reaction monitoring analyses of the differentially expressed proteins. The protein–protein interaction (PPI) networks of the three groups (depression-susceptible (Dep-Sus, A), anxiety-susceptible (Anx-Sus, B), and insusceptible (Insus, C)) were constructed based on protein alterations and Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) pathway term enrichments. Proteins/genes were represented by circular nodes. The KEGG pathway is indicated by the rectangle, shaded based on the p value. Higher p value is shown in yellow and lower p value in blue.
