0: BB female Hu sheep were raised under normal conditions at Linqing Animal Husbandry Co., Ltd (Shandong, China).
1: The sheep were divided into three groups-1 month old (H1) (with weights 12.7 kg, 12.38 kg and 12.75 kg), 3 months old, (16.65 kg, 17.52 kg and 18.3 kg) and 8 months old (40.64 kg, 39.86 kg and 40 kg).
2: Three biological replicates were used for each group: H1(H1O1, H1O2 and H1O3), H3 (H3O2, H3O3 and H3O5) and H8 (H8O1, H8O2 and H8O3).
3: The animals were slaughtered by exsanguinations according to a protocol (GB/T 17236-2008) approved by State Administration for Market Regulation and Standardization Administration, following deep anesthesia with tiletamine/zolazepam (Zoletil 50 Vet, Virbac, France) (tiletamine 50 mg/ml and zolazepam 50 mg/ml), at a dose of 0.1 mg/kg of body weight, administered by intramuscular injection.
4: Horizontal bloodletting was performed immediately after fainting, and the bloodletting from stabbing didnt exceed 30 s. The length of the knife is about 5 cm.
5: The bleeding was not less than 5 min.
6: After mechanical depilation, the floating hair and dirt was washed in the clean water pool.
7: The tissues were collected and frozen in liquid nitrogen and stored immediately at  80 C for further RNA extraction.
8: All the experimental procedures were carried out according to authorization granted by the Chinese Ministry of Agriculture 66.
