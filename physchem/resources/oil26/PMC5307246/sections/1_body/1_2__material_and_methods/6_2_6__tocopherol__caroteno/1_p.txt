0: Carotenoids, tocopherol, and sterols were analyzed at Craft Technologies Inc. (Wilson, NC).
1: Tocopherols were separated and quantified by HPLC according to AOCS method Ce 8-89 [ 13].
2: Carotenoids were separated and quantified by reversed-phase HPLC with UV-Vis detection using published methodology by Craft [ 15].
3: Sterols were separated and quantified using GC according to AOCS Official Method Ch 6-91 [ 16].
