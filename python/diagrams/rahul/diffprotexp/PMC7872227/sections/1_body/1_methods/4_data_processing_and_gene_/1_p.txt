0: Raw data (raw reads) of fastq format were firstly processed through in-house perl scripts.
1: In this step, clean data (clean reads) were obtained by removing reads containing adapter, reads containing poly-N and reads of low quality from raw data.
2: At the same time, the Q20, Q30 and GC content of clean data were calculated.
3: All the downstream analyses were based on the clean data with high quality.
