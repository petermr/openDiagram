0: SPSS 19.0 software was used for the statistical analyses.
1: The normality and homogeneity of variances were tested using the Shapiro-Wilks test and Levenes test, respectively.
2: The significant differences were found using one-way analysis of variance (ANOVA), followed by Duncans multiple range tests and Fishers least significant difference post hoc test.
3: Differences with  P < 0.05 were considered to be significant.
