S1 Fig

Agarose gel showing the PCR products from infected and uninfected snails. Snails were screened for trematode infection by PCR designed to amplify the ITS regions. Only a representation of the 1,200 (900 infected and 300 uninfected) snails is shown. Lanes 1–10 shows the amplified ITS region of Opisthorchis viverrini from infected snails, while lanes 11–20 shows the results of uninfected snails. (+) positive control; (-) negative control. (JPG)
