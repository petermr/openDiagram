0: T.  solium cysticerci were washed three times with sterile ISS.
1: In total, 900 parasites were cultured in 900 ml of RPMI 1640 (Gibco, Thermo Fisher Scientific, Waltham, MA) supplemented with 10% FCS (Gibco) and Pen-strep antibiotics (Gibco) for 3 days at 37C under 5% CO 2.
2: The medium was replaced every 24 h to remove host components [ 21] On day 4, RPMI was replaced by DMEM (Gibco) supplemented with L-glutamine (Gibco) and antibiotics (Gibco).
3: The medium was collected every 48 h and replaced by fresh DMEM.
4: The cultures were kept at 37C under 5% CO 2 for 21 days ( Fig 1).
