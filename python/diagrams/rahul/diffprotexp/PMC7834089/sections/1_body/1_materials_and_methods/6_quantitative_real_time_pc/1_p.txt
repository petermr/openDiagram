0: Total RNA, extracted with TRIzol Reagent (Invitrogen, USA), was converted into cDNA with a ReverTra Ace qPCR RT Kit (TOYOBO, JAPAN) according to manufacturers instructions.
1: Real-time polymerase chain reaction (qPCR) was performed with a BIO-RAD Real-Time Detection System (Bio-rad, USA) using iQ SYBR Green Supermix (Bio-rad, USA).
2: Calculation of mRNA expression levels was performed using the  2CT method and normalized against GAPDH levels.
3: All reactions were run in triplicate.
4: The primer sequences are listed in  Supplemental material (Table 1.
