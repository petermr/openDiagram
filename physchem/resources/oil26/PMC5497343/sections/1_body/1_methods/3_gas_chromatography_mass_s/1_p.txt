0: GC-MS analyses of the essential oil was performed on Agilent 5977A MSD and 7890B GC system, Chemetrix (pty) Ltd.; Agilent Technologies, DE (Germany) with a Zebron-5MS column (ZB-5MS 30 m  0.25 mm  0.25 um) (5% -phenylmethylpolysiloxane).
1: The following column and temperature conditions used were: GC grade helium at a flow rate of 2 mL/min and splitless 1 mL injections was used.
2: The injector, source and oven temperatures were set at 280 C, 280 C and 70 C, respectively.
3: The ramp settings were set at; 15 C/min to 120 C, afterwards 10 C/min to 180 C, then 20 C/min to 270 C and held for 3 min.
