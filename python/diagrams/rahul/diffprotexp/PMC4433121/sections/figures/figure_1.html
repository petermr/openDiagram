Macroscopical and histopathological features of a cPTC. ( A) A mural nodule diagnosed as a cPTC (arrow) in the right lobe of a resected thyroid specimen. ( B) Histopathological slide illustrating the same mural nodule (arrow) after H&E staining exhibiting histopathological features of PTC, spots of calcifications and partially fibrous capsule of thyroid cyst. The sample is shown in x7 (left) and x110 (right) magnifications. ( C) Immunohistochemistry showing expression of thyroglobulin in cPTC tumor cells (magnification x40).
