0: Strains were grown overnight in TSB or TSB supplemented with antibiotic as necessary at 37C with aeration, and the A 600 of the full growth was adjusted to 9.0.
1: Five ml of OD adjusted overnight culture was centrifuged to collect the cells.
2: The pellets were washed with PBS and suspended in sodium dodecyl sulfate-polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis (SDS-PAGE) sample buffer and centrifuged.
3: The supernatant was then boiled for 5 minutes, and 20 l was loaded to a 420% gradient Mini-PROTEANTGXTM precast gel (Bio-Rad Laboratories, Inc., Hercules, CA, USA).
4: After electrophoresis, the gel was stained with Coomassie brilliant blue for visualization.
