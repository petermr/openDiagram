KEGG map ( modified from https://www.genome.jp/dbget-bin/www_bget?path:mmu04261) of the differential expression of adrenergic signaling in cardiomyocytes (ASC) genes from: ( a) the left ventricle vs left atrium and ( b) the right ventricle vs right atrium. Red/green/yellow background of the gene symbol indicates up-/down-/not regulated.
