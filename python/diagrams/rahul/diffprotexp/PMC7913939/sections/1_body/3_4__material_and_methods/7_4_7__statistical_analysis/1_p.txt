0: Results are presented as means  SEM (standard error of the mean).
1: Statistical analysis was performed using  GraphPad Prism 8 software.
2: Normality was tested with the  KolmogorovSmirnov test.
3: Depending on the result of the normality test, an unpaired Students  t-test or an unpaired  t-test with  Welchs correction (when equal variance was not assumed) was used.
4: Moreover, we performed an animal experimentation sample size calculation to estimate the number of animals required to attain statistical significance.
5: Significant differences are indicated for  p-values of<0.05,<0.01, or<0.001.
6: All qRT-PCR and western blotting experiments were performed in triplicates.
