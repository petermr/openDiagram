0: Raw reads of fast-q format were firstly processed through in-house perl scripts to obtain clean reads.
1: At the same time, Q20, Q30 and GC content of the clean data were calculated.
2: All downstream analyses were based on the high quality clean reads.
3: HISAT2 v. 2.0.4 was used to align paired-end clean reads of each sample to sheep reference genome  Oar v. 4.0 [ 46].
4: The mapped reads of each sample were assembled by StringTie v. 1.3.1 [ 46].
