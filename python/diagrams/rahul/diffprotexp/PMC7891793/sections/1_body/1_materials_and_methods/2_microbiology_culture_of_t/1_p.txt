0: For each animal a swab was taken from a mainstem bronchus and one was taken from lung, by searing the outside of the tissue and inserting a sterile swab into the tissue beneath.
1: The swab was rotated in the tissue and then carefully placed in a sterile container with transport media for culture.
2: The samples were inoculated onto diagnostic media at the California Animal Health and Food Safety Diagnostic Laboratory at the University of California, Davis.
3: Samples had aerobic bacterial culture performed and results were reported after 48 hours of incubation.
