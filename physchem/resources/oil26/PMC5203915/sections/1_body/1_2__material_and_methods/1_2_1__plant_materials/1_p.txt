0: Hairy roots (HR) and the roots of soil-grown plants (SGR) of  R. carthamoides were used as the materials.
1: The transformation procedure, the establishment of hairy root cultures, and the obtainment of the roots of soil-grown plants have been described in our previous study [ 11].
2: The hairy roots were grown in liquid Woody Plant medium [ 13] on a rotary shaker at 80 rpm, under a 16/8 h light/dark photoperiod with a light intensity of 40  mol m 2 s 1.
