(B) Proposed uptake of a long chain n-alkane by the identified proteins. TonB interacts with TOL_0244 which carries out high-affinity binding and energy dependent uptake of the substrate into the periplasm. In the absence of TonB, the receptor will bind the substrate but not carry out active transport. TOL_1625 is a FadL homolog which allows hydrophobic compounds to cross the cell membrane through diffusion. TOL_3188 is a porin which may facilitate passive uptake of long-chain alkanes and may be transported to the outer membrane by the inner-membrane transport protein TOL_3187.
