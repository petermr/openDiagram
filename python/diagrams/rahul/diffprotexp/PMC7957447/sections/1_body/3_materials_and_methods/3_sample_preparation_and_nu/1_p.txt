0: Blood sample collection, blood cell separation, blood cell preservation, and serum preservation followed previous study [ 21].
1: After standard diagnostic tests, all anticoagulated venous blood samples treated with ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid disodium salt (EDTA-2Na) were separated by centrifugation at 3000 rpm, then rested at room temperature for 7 min.
2: Whole blood cells were storage at  80 C. Before stored at  80 C, 200-L aliquot of serum mixing with 800 L of ice-cold methanol and a 200-L aliquot of serum mixing with 800 L of ice-cold isopropanol were prepared.
