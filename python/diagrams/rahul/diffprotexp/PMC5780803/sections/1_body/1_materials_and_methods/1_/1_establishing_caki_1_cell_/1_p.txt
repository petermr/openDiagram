0: Caki-1 cells were purchased from China Infrastructure of Cell Line Resources (Beijing, China).
1: The cells were grown in minimum essential medium (Gibco; Thermo Fisher Scientific, Inc., Waltham, MA, USA) supplemented with 10% fetal bovine serum (FBS; Zhejiang Kangyuan Biological Technology Co., Ltd., Zhuji, China) and maintained at 37C in a humidified incubator with 5% CO 2.
2: The cells were split twice weekly and cells that were in the logarithmic growth phase were used for experiments.
