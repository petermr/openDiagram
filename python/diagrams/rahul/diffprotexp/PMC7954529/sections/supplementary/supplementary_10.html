Supplementary file 5.

Metadata for RNA-sequencing data. Column names as described – Species: Cell line species, Lines: Cell line ID, Collection_person: Author initial for who processed cell harvest and nuclear isolation, UndilutedAverage: Average of two cell count measurements 1 × 10 6, AverageAlive: Average of two cell live dead counts – calculated with trypan blue stain, CollectionDate: Date of cell harvest and nuclear isolation, Extraction: Date of RNA extraction, RIN: RIN score for extracted RNA, BioAConc: RNA concentration (ng/µL), Reads: Number of Sequenced reads, Mapped: Number of mapped reads, AssignedOrtho: Number of mapped reads assigned to orthologous exons.
