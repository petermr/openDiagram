0: Statistical analyses were performed as described in 37.
1: One-way ANOVA, followed by a Tukeys HSD test, were used to determine differences in toxicity percentages ( P < 0.05) (SAS for Window release 6, SAS Institute).
2: Log-probit regression was used to determine the LC 50 based on corrected mortality from different chemical concentrations (SAS for Windows release 6, SAS Institute).
3: All analyses were performed in SAS version 9.4.
4: One- or two-way ANOVA was used to determine differences ( P < 0.05) for mRNA quantification and RNA-seq.
5: Graphs were drawn using Prism 8 (GraphPad, Inc., San Diego, CA, USA).
