0: The TCGA database ( https://portal.gdc.cancer.gov/) was used to obtain the data of TUBA1C expression in LUAD tissues and normal tissues.
1: A total of 535 tumor tissues (Additional file  1) and 59 normal tissues were included in the data analysis.
2: Meanwhile, 390 LUAD tissues and 60 normal tissues were treated from the Affiliated Hospital of Nantong University in China.
3: All patients were treated by surgical resection between 2012 and 2013.
4: All clinical data on the patients were carefully recorded after the diagnosis of LUAD by two pathologists.
5: The follow up was 60 months.
6: All experiments involving patient specimens were approved by the Ethics Committee of the Affiliated Hospital of Nantong University, China.
