0: Spinal cord tissue was shattered after pre-cooling with liquid nitrogen and then ground under liquid nitrogen flow.
1: Subsequently, spinal cord tissue was processed with 20 L/mL cocktail protease inhibitor.
2: After addition of lysis buffer, the suspensions were homogenized by sonication using a Dounce homogenizer in iced water and then pure water, and centrifuged.
3: The supernatant was used to determine the protein concentration and then stored at 80C.
