0: This was a single-institution prospective observational study performed at the Texas Childrens Hospital Heart Center.
1: The study was approved by the Institutional Review Board (IRB) of Baylor College of Medicine.
2: All experiments were performed according to the guidelines approved by IRB.
3: Since the subjects were minor, an informed consent was obtained from legal guardian/parent of each enrolled patients.
4: We enrolled 13 neonates diagnosed with HLHS, between July 2016 and August 2017, who were scheduled for a Norwood procedure.
5: Exclusion criteria involved patients with weight < 3 kg at the time of surgery, a known prenatal chromosomal disorder, or gestational age < 34 weeks.
