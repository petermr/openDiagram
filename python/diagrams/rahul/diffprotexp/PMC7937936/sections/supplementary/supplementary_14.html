Supplementary Figure 3

Results of survival analyses in male and female sub-cohorts of TCGA-HNSC. (A, B) No significant correlation between immune score and survival was observed in males or females (P>0.05). (C–E) The expression of all hub genes was significantly correlated with survival in the male group (P<0.005). (F–H) In the females, all hub genes’ expression was not associated with survival (P>0.05).
