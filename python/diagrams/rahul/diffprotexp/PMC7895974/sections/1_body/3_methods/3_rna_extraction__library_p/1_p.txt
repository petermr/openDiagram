0: For ES, MS and DS, consecutive four somites were dissected.
1: Tissues were placed into RLT lysis buffer.
2: RNA was extracted using Qiagen RNAeasy kit (Cat no.
3: 74104) and DNase treated (Qiagen Cat no 79254) for removal of DNA.
4: Libraries were prepared and sequenced on the Illumina HiSeq4000 platform (75 bp paired end) at the Earlham Institute.
5: A minimum of three biological replicates for each stage were used for analysis.
