0: In December 2019, a new strain of coronavirus associated with severe acute respiratory syndrome was identified as SARS-CoV-2.
1: 1 This is an encapsulated, single-stranded RNA virus from the  Coronaviridae family, presenting high virulence and generating a significant global impact.
2: 2 The COVID-19 pandemic caused by SARS-CoV-2 had as its epicenter the city of Wuhan, China, and in a short time became a serious public health problem worldwide.
3: 3 As of end-October, it is present in more than 200 countries, accounting for 45 million cases.
4: 4 In the USA, the country with the highest number of deaths, more than 8 million cases and 220,000 deaths have been recorded.
5: 4 The same dramatic outcome of the pandemic can be noticed in Brazil, the country with the second highest number of COVID-19 deaths, with more than 5 million cases and 150,000 deaths.
