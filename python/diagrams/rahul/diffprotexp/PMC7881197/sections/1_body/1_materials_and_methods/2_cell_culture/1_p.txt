0: Primary mammary normal epithelial cells (HUMEC, ATCC PCS-600-010) and MCF7 (ATCC HTB-22) were purchased from the American Type Culture Collection (ATCC, USA).
1: The cells were cultured at 37 C in a 5% CO 2 incubator.
2: HUMEC were maintained in HuMEC Ready Medium containing HuMEC Basal Serum Free Medium, HuMEC Supplement, and bovine pituitary extract (Cat # 12752-010, Thermo Fisher Scientific, USA).
3: MCF7 cells were cultured in DMEM (Cat # 10013CV, Corining, USA) containing 10% fetal bovine serum (FBS, Gibco, USA) and 1% penicillin/streptomycin (P/S, Thermo Fisher, USA).
