0: Twenty TNBC and 20 non-TNBC clinical samples were collected from patients who were primarily diagnosed with breast cancer between 2013 and 2019 at the Affiliated Hospital of Jiangnan University.
1: All clinical samples were diagnosed by senior pathologists, and the proportions of tumor cells were over 85%.
2: The specimens were immediately transferred into the gas phase of liquid nitrogen after removal, and subsequently stored at 80C until further processing.
3: All the patients who were enrolled in the present study had signed informed consent.
