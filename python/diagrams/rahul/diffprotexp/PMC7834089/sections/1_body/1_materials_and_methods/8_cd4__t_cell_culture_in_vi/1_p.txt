0: MACS was used to select CD4 + T cells positively from the spleen of CVB3-induced myocarditis mice on d 7, according to manufacturers instructions.
1: The day of cells separation was defined as d 0.
2: Cells were cultured in twelve-well plates and randomly divided into seven groups: (1) PBS group, (2) Pnu group (30 M), (3) Pnu (30 M) + pyrrolidinedithiocarbamate (PDTC, a selective NF-B inhibitor, Sigma, USA, 50 M) group, (4) Pnu (30 M) + nsc74859 (NSC, a STAT3 inhibitor, MedChem Express, USA, 100 M) group, (5) methyllycaconitine (MLA, a selective 7nAChR antagonist, Sigma, USA, 10 M) group, (6) MLA (10 M) + PDTC (50 M) group, (7) MLA (10 M) + NSC (100 M) group.
3: All drugs were administered once daily on d 1 to 5.
4: Finally, cells were harvested and used for Western blot and flow cytometry analyses.
