0: H9C2 cells were cultured under different conditions, then removed the culture medium and washed twice with PBS, digested by trypsin.
1: The cells were centrifuged at 800 g, and resuspended with 200 L PBS and transferred to a new centrifuge tube.
2: The centrifuge tube was placed in an ice bath and lysised.
3: The ANP concentration was determined according to a Rat ANP ELISA Kit (CUSABIO).
4: All experiments were performed in triplicate.
