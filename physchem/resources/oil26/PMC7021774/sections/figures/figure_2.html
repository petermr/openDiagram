Manhattan plot of GWAS results for ToxB sensitivity in the association mapping panel ( n = 480) using 22,237 SNPs. Markers are ordered according to the ‘NIAB Elite MAGIC’ genetic map (Gardner et al. 2016). The Bonferroni-adjusted P = 0.01 significance threshold (−log 10 P > 6.35) is indicated. The 21 wheat chromosomes, 1A through to 7D, are indicated. Unmapped markers are not shown
