Evaluation of MIRI. (A) Representative images of 2,3,5-triphenyltetrazolium chloride staining (left) and quantitative analysis of infarct size (right) in hearts subjected to Sham and MIRI surgery. Scale bar, 2 mm. (B) Serum cTnI concentrations in rats with Sham or MIRI surgery. Data are presented as the mean ± SEM, n=4 rats per group. (C) Representative images of H&E staining and Masson trichrome staining from rat left ventricles 2 h after MIRI and in sham group. Scale bar, 100 µm. *P<0.05 vs. sham group. H&E, hematoxylin and eosin; MIRI, myocardial ischemia-reperfusion injury.
