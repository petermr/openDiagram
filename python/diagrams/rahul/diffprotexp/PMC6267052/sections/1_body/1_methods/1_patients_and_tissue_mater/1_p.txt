0: Macroscopically normal knee articular cartilage and meniscus samples were obtained from forensic medicine cases at the University Hospital, Oslo, Norway.
1: The collection was approved by the local ethics committee.
2: Articular cartilage and medial menisci from three donors, aged 36 (male), 43 (male), and 41 (female) years with no history of joint disease, were included.
3: Approximately 1  1 cm large knee articular cartilage pieces were taken perpendicular to the cartilage surface from the medial tibial condyle (representing full-depth cartilage).
4: Full-depth tissue pieces of the medial meniscus were taken from the body region, including both inner and outer regions (synovium and fat was removed).
